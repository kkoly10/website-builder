How it works

Three weeks. No surprises.

/ 01 — WEEK 1

Tell us about your business

You fill out a 10-minute intake. We send back a written scope, a fixed estimate, and a deposit link — usually within 48 hours. No sales calls unless you want one.

/ 02 — WEEK 2

We build it

You watch the site come together inside your workspace. Leave comments on any section, any page, any time. We answer in hours, not days.

We connect your domain, set up analytics, and hand you the keys. The code, the content, the data — all yours. Cancel us anytime, the site is still yours.
